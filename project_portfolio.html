Healthcare Access Initiative: Summer Youth Program with Digital Girl Inc.

As part of the Summer Youth Program in collaboration with Digital Girl Inc., I worked with a team of pre-teens to tackle the issue of affordable healthcare access for underserved communities. Our solution focused on improving accessibility through digital health platforms, community outreach, and cost-effective solutions.

We developed a budgeting plan to estimate costs for healthcare services and transportation, and integrated virtual consultations to help bridge gaps in areas with limited healthcare facilities. The project aimed to make healthcare more affordable and accessible, and we presented our plan to a panel, receiving positive feedback for its feasibility.

Skills Developed:

  • Team Collaboration: Effective communication and idea-sharing
  • Problem-Solving: Overcoming challenges and refining our solution
  • Budgeting and Planning: Developing financial plans for large-scale projects

Text-Based Adventure Game: Brooklyn STEAM Center

At the Brooklyn STEAM Center, I worked on my first Python programming project, creating a text-based adventure game. This was my introduction to coding, and I thoroughly enjoyed learning Python's structure while adding my own creative twists to the game.

For the project, I designed a horror-themed game where the player’s character is kidnapped and must escape a haunted house. Players are presented with various choices, and each decision impacts the outcome—make the wrong choice, and it could lead to death.

Key Highlights:

  • Python Programming: Gained hands-on experience with Python syntax, logic, and structure.
  • Game Design: Developed a branching narrative with multiple outcomes based on player choices.
  • Problem-Solving: Overcame coding challenges related to branching scenarios and user input.

Email Phishing Solution: ISP WBL Challenge with Penguin Random House

During the ISP WBL challenge with Penguin Random House, I collaborated with a team to combat email phishing tactics used by cybercriminals. We developed a solution that focused on creating identification phrases to verify the sender's identity in emails, helping to prevent phishing attacks.

We conducted a thorough SWOT analysis to evaluate the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of our approach, while also designing flowcharts to outline the process. We incorporated client feedback to refine and enhance our solution, ensuring its practicality and effectiveness.

Key Highlights:

  • Problem-Solving: Developed a creative solution to combat email phishing.
  • Analysis and Strategy: Performed SWOT analysis and created detailed flowcharts.
  • Client Collaboration: Incorporated client feedback to improve the solution.
